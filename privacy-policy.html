Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 1, 2025

ChatGPT Kit is a Chrome extension designed to help you organize your ChatGPT conversations. We take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines what data we collect (if any), how it's used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

By default, ChatGPT Kit does not collect any personal data. All chat links, labels, and organization features are stored locally in your browser.

However, if you choose to sign up for a premium account in the future, we may collect:

  • Your email address (for account login)
  • Saved chat data (e.g., hyperlinks, labels) stored in our database to sync across devices

2. How We Use Your Data

If you sign up, your email and chat data will be used solely to provide and improve the service, including syncing your data and managing your subscription.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Security

We use secure methods to store and manage your data. All communication between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS.

4. Local Storage

If you're using the free version without signing in, your data is saved only on your local device and never leaves your browser.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as Supabase and Stripe for authentication and billing. These services have their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to delete your data at any time. If you are a premium user, you can request full deletion of your account by contacting us at info@adam-solutions.io.

7. Contact

If you have any questions, please reach out to us at info@adam-solutions.io.
